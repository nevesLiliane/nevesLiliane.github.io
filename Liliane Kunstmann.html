PhD in Systems and Computer Engineering with solid experience in software development, data science, and high-performance computing (HPC). My passion lies in transforming complex challenges into efficient, scalable, and high-quality solutions.

I actively seek challenging opportunities in applied research, systems development, or technology project management where I can apply my academic knowledge, my practical skills in Python, AngularJS and Java, and my passion for innovative solutions to drive results and growth.
